ACTIVATING JOBPRO DIRECTORY

JobPro requires activation for full directory access beyond any trial period. We will provide you with your purchased activation code(s) once you install the JobPro Directory and email us your activation request(s).

The following assumes you have an active internet connection and will be emailing us your activation request.

1) Launch the JobPro Directory by double-clicking its desktop icon.

2) Click on the program's Activate Program menu.

3) Enter your name as Registered Owner on the program's Activation form.

4) Click the Activate by Email button on the Activation form to send us your product code (looks like J1.285 AT4202 2084 Win7 3059 Company Name). Then, open your email program (Outlook, etc.) and click Send/Receive to make sure your activation request is sent.

Then, once you receive an activation code from us ...
5 ) Enter the activation code in step 4 on the Activation form.

6) Click Activate Now (step 5 on the Activation form).
That's it! You have successfully activated the JobPro BC Employers Directory.
